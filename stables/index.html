About Maggie's Stables

Maggie's Stables is a family run facility which is located in Utah. Wether you're looking to go on a scenic horseback ride or wanting to take lessons, we offer both at affordable prices.

You can join us each day for horseback rides on beautiful Utah trails. Each day we offer three times to go on a beautiful horseback ride on two different trails. Two of the times we offer are one hour rides and the third is two hours.

Not only do we do rides, but we also offer lessons for all levels. All riders from beginners to experienced are welcome to come and learn at our facility.

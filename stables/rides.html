Important Information

Below is some important information for you to know.

Weight Limit
Know there is a weight limit to insure the safety and comfort of both riders and horses.
Children
Children under 16 years of age much be accompanied by an adult.
Age Requirement
To go on rides, riders must be at least eight years or older.
